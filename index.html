spend by squaremelon

spend is a web application for helping to budget your monthly expenses. We came up with this idea after realising that payment models for a lot of things in our lives are now moving over to subscriptions.

To combat this issue, we are creating a really simple interface to calculate how much money you have coming in per month, and then help you to divide that into savings, subscriptions and other kinds of bills.
